## 🔬 Research Directions

- Diffusion & Visual Generation — acceleration, controllable generation, object removal, poster synthesis
- Multimodal Reasoning & MoE — routing analysis, cross-modal intervention, expert activation
- LLM Controllability & Steering — unified frameworks for parameter dynamics, behavioral evaluation
- AI Safety — multimodal adversarial robustness, safety benchmarking
- AI for Security — large language models for cybersecurity, domain-specific data synthesis and training
- Intelligent Vulnerability Analysis & Exploitation — automated vulnerability detection, analysis, and exploitation
- Intelligent Attack-Defense Adversarial — automated penetration testing, red-blue teaming, and adversarial simulation
